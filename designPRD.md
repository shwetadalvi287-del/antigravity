# STEM Quest Rural

## Product Requirements Document (PRD) + Functional Requirements Document (FRD)

Version: 1.0
Product Type: EdTech / Gamified Learning Platform
Target Users: Rural Students (Class 6–12)

---

# 1. Product Overview

STEM Quest Rural is a gamified learning platform designed for students in rural areas from **Class 6 to Class 12**.

The platform aims to solve one major problem:

> Rural students often fear STEM subjects and gradually lose interest.

STEM Quest transforms learning into a game-like experience using:

* Missions
* Challenges
* Rewards
* Leaderboards
* AI Tutor
* Virtual Labs
* Personalized Learning

The goal is to make STEM:

* Fun
* Fear-free
* Interactive
* Accessible
