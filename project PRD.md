# PRD — Gamification Study Platform for Rural STEM Education

## 1. Product Name

**STEM Quest**
(A gamified learning platform for rural students to make STEM fun and fear-free)

---

## 2. Problem Statement

Students in rural areas (Class 6–12) often face difficulties in learning STEM subjects:

* Fear of difficult subjects like **Math, Physics, Chemistry**
* Lack of quality teachers/resources
* Learning feels boring and theoretical
* Low confidence in solving problems
* Fear of failure in exams
* Limited access to practical experiments/labs

Because of this, many students lose interest in STEM and avoid science-related careers.

---

## 3. Vision

To transform STEM education in rural areas by making learning:

* Fun
* Interactive
* Game-based
* Reward-driven
* Easy to understand

Goal:
Help students overcome fear and develop genuine interest in STEM.

---

## 4. Target Users

### Primary Users

Students from **Class 6–12** in rural schools.

Age Group:

* 11–18 years

### Secondary Users

* Teachers
* Parents
* Schools / NGOs

---

## 5. Objectives

Main objectives of the platform:

### Learning Goals

* Improve STEM understanding
* Increase curiosity
* Reduce subject fear
* Improve exam performance
* Encourage practical thinking

### Product Goals

* Daily active usage
* Higher lesson completion rate
* Better quiz scores
* Long-term STEM engagement

---

## 6. User Pain Points

| Problem          | Effect            |
| ---------------- | ----------------- |
| STEM feels hard  | Students avoid it |
| No motivation    | Low engagement    |
| Fear of mistakes | No practice       |
| Boring lectures  | Loss of interest  |
| Weak basics      | Future difficulty |

---

## 7. Proposed Solution

A mobile-first/web platform using **gamification**.

Learning becomes like a game:

* Complete missions
* Solve puzzles
* Earn coins
* Unlock levels
* Compete with friends
* Get rewards

Instead of:

> “Study Chapter 5”

Students see:

> “Mission 5: Save the Rocket using Newton’s Laws 🚀”

This changes psychology from **fear → excitement**.

---

## 8. Core Features

# 8.1 Gamified Lessons

Every chapter becomes a mission.

Examples:

### Math

* Algebra Battle
* Geometry Puzzle Quest

### Physics

* Gravity Challenge
* Circuit Builder

### Chemistry

* Molecule Matching Game
* Chemical Reaction Lab

### Biology

* Cell Explorer
* Human Body Adventure

---

# 8.2 Level System

Students progress through levels.

Example:

* Beginner
* Explorer
* Solver
* Innovator
* STEM Master

Each completed lesson = XP (Experience Points)

---

# 8.3 Reward System

Students earn:

* Coins
* Badges
* Stars
* Trophies

Examples:
🏅 Math Hero
🏅 Physics Warrior
🏅 Problem Solver

Rewards motivate regular study.

---

# 8.4 Daily Challenges

Short 5–10 minute challenges.

Examples:

* Solve 3 equations
* Identify 5 chemical symbols
* Build electric circuit

Purpose:
Build habit of daily learning.

---

# 8.5 Leaderboard

Friendly competition between:

* Classmates
* Village schools
* District schools

This increases motivation.

---

# 8.6 Hint System

When students get stuck:

* Step-by-step hints
* Easy explanations
* Visual diagrams

This reduces fear.

---

# 8.7 AI Tutor

AI assistant explains concepts simply.

Example:
Student asks:

> Why is algebra difficult?

AI explains in local language with simple examples.

Could support:

* English
* Marathi
* Hindi

---

## 9. Gamification Mechanics

### Points

Earn for:

* Correct answers
* Streaks
* Fast solving

### Badges

Given for achievements.

### Streaks

Example:
7 days study streak → reward

### Unlockables

New chapters unlocked after progress.

### Boss Battles

End-of-chapter mega quiz.

Example:
“Defeat Algebra Boss”

Very engaging for students.
